# Smart Borehole Safety System

## 📖 Overview

The **Smart Borehole Safety System** is an IoT-based safety solution designed to prevent accidents caused by open or abandoned boreholes. The system continuously monitors movement around the borehole using PIR sensors, automatically controls a protective safety cover, and sends real-time alerts through an ESP32-based IoT platform.

This project aims to improve public safety by providing intelligent monitoring, automatic protection, and timely notifications in rural areas, construction sites, and agricultural fields.

---

## 📝 Abstract

Unprotected boreholes are a serious safety hazard and can cause accidents to people and animals, especially in rural areas and construction sites. Traditional safety methods, such as manual covers and occasional monitoring, are unreliable and cannot respond quickly during emergencies.

To address this problem, a **Smart Borehole Safety System** is proposed, providing continuous monitoring, automatic protection, and intelligent response.

The system consists of two layers:

- **Outer Sensing Layer:** Four PIR sensors are placed around the borehole to detect movement from all directions. The ESP32 processes sensor data and sends instant alerts through an IoT platform.

- **Inner Protection Layer:** A servo motor automatically opens or closes the safety cover based on detected movement. Manual push buttons are provided for emergency operation and maintenance.

Additional features include motion-triggered camera capture, night safety mode, and a risk assessment system that classifies activity into low, medium, or high risk.

The proposed system is cost-effective, scalable, and suitable for real-time deployment.

## ⚙️ Working

1. PIR sensors continuously monitor all four sides of the borehole.
2. Motion is detected from any direction.
3. ESP32 processes the sensor data.
4. Risk level is identified.
5. Servo motor automatically operates the safety cover.
6. IoT alerts are sent to authorized users.
7. Camera captures images when movement is detected.
8. Manual buttons allow emergency operation.
